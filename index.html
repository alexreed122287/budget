Sync & sharing
Where do I get the Web app URL to share with my spouse?
  1. Create a Google Sheet (any blank one).
  2. In it: Extensions → Apps Script.
  3. Delete the sample code, paste the contents of apps-script.gs from the project repo, and Save.
  4. Click Deploy → New deployment → type Web app.
  5. Set Execute as: Me and Who has access: Anyone, then Deploy and authorize.
  6. Copy the Web app URL it gives you (ends in /exec) and paste it below, then Save.
  7. Share that one URL with your spouse — they paste it into this same box on their device. You both now read/write the same data, so the calendar & everything stays in sync.
Treat the URL like a password — anyone with it can see your data. Only share it with your spouse.

Day: for monthly / quarterly / yearly = day of month (1–31). For weekly / biweekly = day of week (0=Sun … 4=Thu … 6=Sat). For biweekly, set the anchor date in your local data if needed; defaults to start of the current month.
Income — Partner Share (quarterly bonus)
Quarterly bonuses paid in addition to the regular Thursday paycheck. Enter the amount and pick up to five receipt dates below. Each date gets its own ★ gold-highlighted column on the Budget tab. If a receipt date lands on a regular paycheck day, the two amounts combine into one column. You can fine-tune any single payment by clicking its cell on the Budget tab (a per-date override).
Leave a slot blank if unused. Clear a date to remove that payment from the budget.

Travel pricing (live flights & hotels)
Real flight & hotel prices on the Vacation tab, from the free Amadeus Self-Service API. Amadeus needs a secret key that can't be exposed on a public page, so prices route through a tiny Cloudflare Worker you deploy once (see AMADEUS.md — paste amadeus-worker.js, add your Amadeus key/secret as Worker variables). Then paste the Worker URL below. Leave blank to keep using AI ballpark estimates.
Push notifications
Real OS notifications that fire even when the app is closed — for To-Do reminders and Calendar events. Requires one-time setup (see PUSH.md): generate a VAPID key pair with ./make-vapid.sh, deploy push-worker.js as a Cloudflare Worker, then paste the Worker URL + public key below and tap Enable. On iPhone the app must be added to your Home Screen first.
