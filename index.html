▣ BILLS LIBRARY — recurring rules
NAME AMOUNT FREQUENCY DUE DAY CATEGORY ACTIVE
FREQUENCY OPTIONS:
• Monthly: paid on the same day every month (Due Day = 1–31).
• Weekly: paid every week on a specific weekday (Due Day = 0=Sun, 1=Mon, … 6=Sat).
• Biweekly: every 2 weeks from the date you set in Notes (or the start of the month if blank).
• Quarterly: every 3 months on Due Day.
• Semi-annual: every 6 months on Due Day.
• Yearly: once a year on Due Day (use Notes field MM for the month, e.g. 06 for June).
• Once: a single one-off charge (use Notes field YYYY-MM-DD).
